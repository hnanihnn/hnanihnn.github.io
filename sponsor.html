Want To Sponsor A Pet?

Wiggling Paws offer a monthly sponsorship program for those animal lovers who cannot bring them home. Sponsors will surely make the animals' lives a lot better!

Monthly sponsors are encouraged to visit the shelter regularly for the sponsored animal to be comfortable with you.

You can choose whichever pet that you would like to sponsor:
Cats or Dogs

Sponsorship FAQs



How can I sponsor a specific animal?

> In Person: Visit us anytime during our operating hours to fill in a sponsorship form.
> Online: Scroll down below to fill in and submit the online sponsorship form.

How much is the monthly sponsorship fee of an animal?

> Adult Cats and Dogs (above 1 year): RM80.00
> Kittens and Puppies (below 1 year): RM60.00

What is my sponsorship fee used for?

Your sponsorship fee will be used to purchase your sponsored animal's food and medication, provide for neutering as well as help meet other day-to-day expenses. It will also be pooled to provide assistance and benefit to all our animals and improve the general environment at the shelter.

What are the benefits of being a monthly sponsor?

Monthly sponsors will receive a personalized certificate and colour picture of your sponsored animal, a letter of appreciation and daily updates on the wellbeing of their sponsored animal.

How often can I visit my sponsored animal?

You can visit them as many times as you like during our operational hours. We encourage sponsors to spend a lot of time with the animals so that they can have company.
