Wiggling Paws is a non-profit organization that provide
shelter to animals such as dogs and cats.

Our aim is to build a community in which dogs and cats
can live free from suffering or cruelty.
We focus on providing the best possible welfare for the
animals in our care.



Our shelter receives unwanted dogs and cats which we
will vaccinate, deworm, spray and put up for adoption.

If anyone is interested with the cats
and dogs at our shelter, they can apply for an adoption.



The services that we offered for this animal shelter are
volunteering and surrender your pet.
People who are interested to be a volunteer, they can
apply for the job online.

And people can surrender their pets if they cannot
keep their pets anymore.



People who are willing to donate for the dogs and cats,
they can do it once or even monthly.

And those who want to have a pet but could not save
them at home can sponsor a pet at the shelter.
They are encouraged to come and visit the pet regularly
to give them attention and care that the pets need.
