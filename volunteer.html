We Need Volunteers. Just Like You.

Volunteers are the backbone of our facility and aid our staff in daily operations therefore we need animal lovers like you to help us in our mission to safeguard, rescue, heal, adopt and advocate for animals. At Wiggling Paws, volunteers help to care for the animals and operate our programs in a variety of different areas. Read more about our Volunteer Roles and Volunteer Requirement and then fill in the Volunteer Recruitment Form to join us!

Volunteer Requirement


AGE REQUIREMENT

Volunteers must be above the age of 13

TIME COMMITMENT

Must commit to at least one shift every other week. Shifts are 2-4 hours depending on assignment

RULES AND REGULATIONS

Volunteers must read and comprehend detailed policy and protocol information and remember and follow shelter rules.

RESPONSIBILITIES

Volunteers must understand and fulfill all responsibilities of their role independently.

DRESSING

Volunteers must be dressed appropriately. The recommended attire for volunteering is a comfortable t-shirt, long pants and closed-toe shoes for your safety.

PHYSICAL REQUIREMENT

Physically fit enough to stand and or walk for up to 3 hours and be able to lift and carry 8kg or more.

Volunteering Roles

Dog Walker

Volunteers provide dogs with bathroom breaks,exercise and socialization.

Animal bather

Volunteers bathe and groom animals available for adoption to help keep them healthy and in good appearance for potential adopters.

Dog Reading Volunteer

While sitting outside dog's kennel, volunteers can read to or play music to the dogs to soothe them and adapt them to positive human presence.

Kitten Socialization Volunteer

In a medical environment, interact with kittens under 8 weeks of age and assist in the socialization process through interactive play, petting and soothing, encouraging them to eat, and ensuring that all kittens receive care and attention.

Cat Adoption Support

Volunteers working as Cat Adoption Support work in the cat area and focus on helping customers with cats and help clicker training cats.

Surgical Prep Assistant

Volunteers prepare materials for surgery and help care for animals during recovery to provide support in order to efficiently Spay/Neuter pets who are surrendered to the Wiggling Paws.
