Want to Surrender a Pet?

Wiggling Paws helps hundreds of dogs, cats and help pets find loving homes each year. Surrendering your pet to a sheltering agency should be considered a last resort. Therefore, anyone who wishes to surrender their pet to us will have to undergo a series of process whereby we will evaluate if the dogs or cats are eligible to be surrendered to us.


How to Surrender your Pet?


Step 1

Talk your decision over with our Surrender Counselor.

Please call the Surrender Department at +60113674123 in order to discuss your decision. If you're unable to speak directly with our counselor please leave a message here.


Step 2

Make An Appointment.

We take in surrenders by appointment only. This appointment system allows us to manage the amount of pet surrendered to us. Our surrender counselor will try their best to discuss your availability to surrender and find a time that works for both you and our organization.


Step 3

Arrive at your scheduled appointment time and date.

You will receive a phone call 1-2 days before your appointment to confirm. At this time please let us know about any cancellations or if you need to reschedule.


Step 4

Meet With a Surrender Counselor.

Our Surrender Counselor will sit down with you to go over the process of leaving an animal. If the animal is an owner surrender you will be required to fill out a personality profile on the animal during your counseling session.
