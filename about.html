OUR STORY

Wiggling Paws society is an animal shelter located in Bangsar has been in operation since 2017. Wiggling Paws is a non-profit organization that provide shelter to animals such as dogs and cats. Our aim is to build a community in which dogs and cats can live free from suffering or cruelty. We focus on providing the best possible welfare for the animals in our care.

We strive to provide all aspects of care to abandoned, neglected, and injured animals; reunite lost pets with their caregivers or seek new families for them; reinforce a standard of living for animals and prevent cruelty. The shelter and all costs involved in running it are entirely funded by the generous donations of the public as well as proceeds from charitable events.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

1. What is Wiggling Paws?
Wiggling Paws is a non-profit organization that provide shelter to animals such as dogs and cats. We help to find abandoned cats and dogs their new home.

2. Where is Wiggling Paws located?
Wiggling Paws is located at Bangsar.

3. When is the operating hour?
We are opened Wednesdays-Mondays from 11am-7pm and we are closed on Tuesdays.

4. Do you accept any animals apart from cats and dogs?
No, our animal shelter are specifically for cats and dogs and we do not impose a time limit on an animal’s stay and strive to find new ways to treat animals with medical or behavior problems.

5. What is the adoption process and where can I apply for an adoption?
You need to visit our place, choose which one that you wanted to adopt and go for an interview with our staffs. There is no application form that need to be completed before you visit.

6. Where can I apply for volunteer?
You need to apply the form at our volunteer page or apply normally at our place!

7. How can I make some donation in terms of money or maybe some physical stuffs?
You can make the donation at our donate page or you can buy items on our wishlist and deliver it at our doorstep!
